## 📊 Project 2: Wholesale Customer Revenue Analysis | SQL + Excel

**Tools Used:** MySQL Workbench, Microsoft Excel, Data Visualization

**Business Problem:** Analyzed 440 wholesale customer records to identify key revenue drivers across channels, product categories, and regions.

**SQL Techniques Applied:**
- `GROUP BY` & `ORDER BY` for customer segmentation
- `UNION ALL` to combine multi-category revenue results
- `IF` Statements for data transformation: Channel & Region mapping
- `SUM()`, `COUNT()` Aggregate Functions for KPI calculation

**Key Business Insights:**
| **Analysis** | **Finding** | **Business Recommendation** |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Channel** | Horeca drives 55% revenue vs Retail 45% | Strengthen B2B restaurant partnerships |
| **Category** | Fresh Products: 5.2M sales | Increase Fresh inventory by 40% |
| **Region** | Other Regions: 73% share with 316 customers | Expand distribution network |
| **Top Customer** | ID 86: $199K annual spend | Launch VIP retention program |
